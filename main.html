Paris


Paris, the capital city of France, is renowned for its art, fashion, and culture. Known as the "City of Light", Paris offers a unique blend of historical and modern attractions that make it a favorite destination for travelers worldwide.

  1. Eiffel Tower - A must-see landmark in Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum - Home to the famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral - An iconic gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées - The most famous avenue in the world.
  5. Sacré-Cœur Basilica - Offers a breathtaking view of the city.
  • French Croissant - A buttery and flaky pastry.
  • Escargot - A dish made from edible land snails.
  • Baguette - A traditional long, thin loaf of French bread.
  • Macarons - Sweet meringue-based confection.
  • Ratatouille - A stewed vegetable dish.
